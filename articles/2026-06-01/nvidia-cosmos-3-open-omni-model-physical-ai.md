## Summary & Key Takeaways

- NVIDIA has launched Cosmos 3, an 'open omni-model'.
- It is designed for physical AI reasoning and action.
- Cosmos 3 aims to advance embodied AI and robotics capabilities.
- This model represents a significant step towards more intelligent physical systems.
- It combines various modalities for comprehensive understanding and interaction.

## Our Commentary

NVIDIA is really leaning into the physical AI space, and 'omni-model' sounds incredibly ambitious. The idea of a single model for physical reasoning and action is a huge leap. We're moving beyond just text and images; this is about AI interacting with the real world. It's exciting and a little terrifying to imagine the implications for robotics and automation. The future is going to be very, very physical.

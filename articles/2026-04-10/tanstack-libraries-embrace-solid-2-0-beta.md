## Summary & Key Takeaways

- TanStack Router, Start, and Query have officially added beta support for Solid 2.0.
- This integration allows developers to begin experimenting with Solid's upcoming major release in real-world applications.
- The move signifies growing ecosystem support for SolidJS as it evolves.

This is great news for the SolidJS community! TanStack libraries are foundational for many web applications, and getting early beta support for Solid 2.0 means developers can start testing and providing feedback on the next iteration of SolidJS. It's a strong signal of confidence in Solid's future and will help ensure a smoother transition when 2.0 officially drops.

## Summary & Key Takeaways

- Cypress has released a new guide on "Working with AI Agents" to help teams accelerate accessibility workflows.
- Cypress Accessibility operates in the background within Cypress Cloud, automatically capturing application state snapshots during test runs.
- These snapshots are processed with accessibility checks, generating valuable data.
- This accessibility data is now available through Cypress Cloud MCP, providing easy access to specific pages, rules, and elements with issues.
- The guide aims to show how AI agents can utilize this data to further enhance and automate accessibility testing.

## Our Commentary

The intersection of AI and accessibility testing is genuinely exciting. Cypress making this data available via Cloud MCP and providing guidance on AI agents could be a significant step for teams struggling with comprehensive accessibility coverage. It's not just about finding issues, but potentially automating the analysis and even suggesting fixes. We're keen to see how this evolves and if it truly makes a dent in the often-overlooked area of web accessibility.

## Summary & Key Takeaways

- `z-index` behavior is governed by CSS stacking contexts.
- Understanding stacking contexts is crucial for element layering.
- Elements within a lower stacking context cannot appear above elements in a higher one.
- The article explains common pitfalls and how to avoid them.

## Summary & Key Takeaways

- Google is transitioning Gemini CLI to Antigravity CLI.
- Antigravity CLI is a new agent-first platform for multi-agent workflows.
- It offers faster execution and asynchronous processing.
- Individual and free users must transition by June 18, 2026.

## Our Commentary

The transition from Gemini CLI to Antigravity CLI is a clear sign of Google's commitment to the agent-first paradigm. Unifying tools is generally a good thing for DX, but I'm always wary of forced migrations, especially with deadlines. It's a necessary step for their ecosystem, but I hope the transition is smooth for developers.

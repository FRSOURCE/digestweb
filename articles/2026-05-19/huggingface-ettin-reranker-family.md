## Summary & Key Takeaways

- Hugging Face introduces the Ettin Reranker Family.
- These models are designed to improve search relevance.
- They are likely beneficial for retrieval augmented generation (RAG).
- This expands Hugging Face's open-source AI offerings.

## Our Commentary

A new reranker family from Hugging Face is always interesting. Rerankers are often the unsung heroes of effective RAG systems, and having more open-source options is a win for the community. I'm curious to see how Ettin performs against existing models.

## Summary & Key Takeaways

- Joint Iteration has reached Stage 4.
- This means it's finalized for inclusion in the next ECMAScript standard.
- It allows for simultaneous iteration over multiple iterables.
- This feature will simplify common data processing patterns.

## Our Commentary

Joint Iteration reaching Stage 4 is fantastic! This is one of those quality-of-life features that will make working with multiple arrays or iterables so much cleaner. No more manual index management or awkward `map`/`filter` chains when you just need to process things in parallel. I'm genuinely excited to use this.

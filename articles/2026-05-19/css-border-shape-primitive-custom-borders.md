## Summary & Key Takeaways

- `border-shape` is an upcoming CSS primitive.
- It defines custom shapes for an element's border.
- This simplifies creating non-standard shapes like speech bubbles or hearts.
- It offers a native alternative to `clip-path` for borders.

## Our Commentary

`border-shape`! Finally!

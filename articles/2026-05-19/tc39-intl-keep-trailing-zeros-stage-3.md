## Summary & Key Takeaways

- The `Intl Keep Trailing Zeros` proposal has reached Stage 3.
- This update was approved at the May 19, 2026 TC39 meeting.
- It enhances `Intl.NumberFormat` for precise decimal display.
- Stage 3 means the proposal is stable and ready for implementations.

## Our Commentary

`Intl Keep Trailing Zeros` hitting Stage 3 is a quiet but important win for internationalization. Dealing with number formatting, especially trailing zeros, can be surprisingly complex. Having a standardized way to handle this directly in `Intl.NumberFormat` will be a relief for many developers working on global applications.

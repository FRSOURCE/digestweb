## Summary & Key Takeaways

- Rhumb Studio is a small, two-person design studio.
- They specialize in crafting immersive and spatial web experiences.
- Their approach aims to make web content feel more like places than traditional pages.
- The studio's work redefines web design through innovative digital interactions.

It's always inspiring to see small studios pushing the boundaries of web design. We often get caught up in frameworks and tooling, but articles like this remind us of the creative potential of the web platform itself. The idea of web experiences feeling more like 'places' than 'pages' is a compelling vision for the future of interaction. It makes me wonder what new paradigms we'll see emerge as these ideas gain traction.

## Summary & Key Takeaways

- AI tools are reducing the need for colleagues to 'bug' each other for help.
- This efficiency, however, replaces informal interactions crucial for team building.
- These interactions are vital for fostering trust, belonging, and innovation within teams.
- The article explores the potential negative impacts and offers strategies for maintaining human connection while leveraging AI.

This article hits on a crucial, often overlooked, aspect of AI adoption: its subtle impact on human interaction and team cohesion. We've been so focused on AI's productivity gains that we might be inadvertently eroding the very social fabric that makes teams effective. It's a powerful reminder that technology isn't just about efficiency; it's about people. We need to be incredibly intentional about designing our AI-augmented workflows to preserve and even enhance human connection. This is a conversation we all need to be having.

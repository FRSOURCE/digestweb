## Summary & Key Takeaways

- OpenAI has introduced Symphony, an open-source specification.
- Symphony is designed for Codex orchestration, enabling AI agents.
- It aims to transform issue trackers into always-on agent systems.
- The goal is to boost engineering output and reduce context switching for developers.

This is genuinely exciting! An open-source spec for AI agent orchestration from OpenAI is a big step towards standardizing how we build and manage these systems. The idea of turning issue trackers into 'always-on agent systems' is powerful – imagine agents autonomously tackling tasks, reducing the mental load on engineers. This could be a game-changer for developer productivity and how we interact with our tools. We're eager to see how the community adopts and extends Symphony.

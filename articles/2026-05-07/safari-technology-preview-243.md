## Summary & Key Takeaways

- Safari Technology Preview 243 is now available for download on macOS Tahoe and macOS Sequoia.
- These releases typically include updates to WebKit's rendering engine, experimental web features, and various bug fixes.
- Developers can use STP to test upcoming browser capabilities and provide feedback to the WebKit team.

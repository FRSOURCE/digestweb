## Summary & Key Takeaways

- The Nielsen Norman Group presents ten key guidelines for designing effective and user-friendly AI chatbots for websites.
- Recommendations include clearly communicating the chatbot's capabilities and limitations to users.
- Designers should provide relevant prompt suggestions to guide user interactions and improve usability.
- Effective chatbots should quickly signal their understanding of the user's current context or query.
- The guidelines aim to enhance the overall user experience and utility of site-specific AI assistants.

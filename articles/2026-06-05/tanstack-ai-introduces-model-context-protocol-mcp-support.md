## Summary & Key Takeaways

- TanStack AI now includes host-side Model Context Protocol (MCP) support.
- Users can connect single MCP servers or a pool of them.
- It offers choices for managed or manual lifecycle control.
- The release provides flexible type-safety levels, from zero-config to fully generated types.

## Our Commentary

TanStack continues to impress with its modular approach. Bringing MCP support to TanStack AI with such flexibility, especially around type-safety, is a smart move. We appreciate the focus on developer choice, whether it's a simple setup or a fully type-safe, generated experience. It feels like they're really thinking about how developers will integrate AI into their existing workflows.

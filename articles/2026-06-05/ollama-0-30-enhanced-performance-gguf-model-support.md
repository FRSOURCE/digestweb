## Summary & Key Takeaways

- Ollama 0.30 is now available, bringing improved performance.
- It introduces enhanced GGUF model compatibility through llama.cpp.
- This update augments Ollama's existing MLX engine on Apple silicon.
- The changes enable support for more models across a wider range of hardware.

## Our Commentary

Ollama continues to be a go-to for local LLM experimentation. Performance boosts and broader GGUF support are always welcome. It's great to see them leveraging llama.cpp and MLX to push the boundaries of what's possible on consumer hardware. We're all about making powerful AI accessible, and Ollama is doing just that.

## Summary & Key Takeaways

- The article introduces the concept of 'Probabilistic Design' for AI-informed UX.
- It encourages designers to embrace uncertainty in AI predictions, not certainty.
- The mindset promotes nuanced interpretation of AI outputs for better decisions.
- It helps UX and product teams make adaptive choices in an evolving AI landscape.
- This approach aims to prevent mistaking AI predictions for absolute truths.

## Our Commentary

'Probabilistic Design' is a concept we absolutely need. The hype around AI often makes it sound like a crystal ball, but it's anything but. Teaching designers to think in probabilities and embrace the messiness of AI outputs is crucial for building truly resilient systems.

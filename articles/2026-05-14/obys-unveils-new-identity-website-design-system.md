## Our Commentary

While not a technical deep-dive into code, this article offers a fascinating glimpse into the design process behind a major agency's rebrand. We often focus on the "how" of development, but the "why" and "what" of design are equally crucial for creating compelling web experiences. Seeing how Obys approached unifying type, motion, and structure into a single system is inspiring. It's a good reminder that the visual and interactive aspects of a website are just as important as its underlying code for delivering a memorable product.

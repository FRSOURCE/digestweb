## Summary & Key Takeaways

- OpenAI's Codex, an AI coding agent, is now integrated into the ChatGPT mobile app.
- This allows users to work with Codex from anywhere, across various devices.
- Developers can monitor, steer, and approve coding tasks in real-time.
- The mobile access aims to enhance flexibility for remote and on-the-go development environments.

## Our Commentary

Making Codex accessible via the ChatGPT mobile app is a smart move for developer experience. While I don't imagine many people will be writing complex code on their phone, the ability to monitor, steer, and approve tasks remotely is incredibly useful. It speaks to the increasing trend of "anywhere, anytime" development and the need for tools to adapt to flexible workflows. It's a small but significant step towards making AI coding assistants more integrated into our daily, often fragmented, work lives.

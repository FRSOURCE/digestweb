## Summary & Key Takeaways

- Cursor, an AI-powered IDE, has introduced new interactive capabilities for its canvas feature.
- Users can now interact directly with visualizations generated by AI agents within the canvases.
- This enhancement aims to improve the developer experience by making AI outputs more dynamic and actionable.
- The feature integrates AI-driven insights more deeply into the development workflow.

## Our Commentary

Interactive visualizations from AI agents in an IDE? That's a neat idea. It's a small but significant step towards making AI outputs more than just static text.

## Summary & Key Takeaways

- The article explores the fundamental purpose of design.
- It argues that design's core job is problem-solving, not just aesthetics.
- Understanding "what problem, why, and for whom" is paramount.
- The piece encourages a deeper, more thoughtful approach to design.

## Our Commentary

"Uber for Dogs" is a great hook for a design philosophy piece. It's so easy to get caught up in the visual aspects of design, but this article reminds us that the real magic happens when you deeply understand the problem you're trying to solve. It's a good reset for anyone in product or design.

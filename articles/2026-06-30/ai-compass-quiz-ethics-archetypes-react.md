## Summary & Key Takeaways

- "The AI Compass" is a political compass-style quiz about AI and AI ethics.
- It categorizes users into one of 30 archetypes based on their answers.
- The quiz is implemented as a single-page React application.
- It uses the `<script type="text/babel">` trick to avoid a build step.
- The project serves as an engaging community-created tool.

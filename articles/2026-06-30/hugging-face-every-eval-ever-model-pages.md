## Summary & Key Takeaways

- Hugging Face is now displaying "Every Eval Ever" results on its model pages.
- This integration aims to improve transparency for AI model performance.
- Developers can more easily access and compare evaluation data.
- The feature enhances the discovery and selection process for models.

## Our Commentary

This is a smart move from Hugging Face. Having evaluation results front and center on model pages is a huge win for transparency and developer trust. It makes comparing models so much easier and helps cut through the hype. We need more of this kind of data-driven approach in the AI ecosystem.

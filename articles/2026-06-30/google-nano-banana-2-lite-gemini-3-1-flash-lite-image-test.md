## Summary & Key Takeaways

• Google has released "Nano Banana 2 Lite," also known as Gemini 3.1 Flash Lite Image.
• This model is marketed as the "fastest and cheapest Gemini image model."
• Simon Willison tested it with a "Where's Waldo" style prompt and found the results promising.
• He noted that the model still made spelling errors in the generated image.

## Our Commentary

"Nano Banana 2 Lite" is a fun name. It's good to see Google pushing for faster, cheaper image models. The spelling errors are a classic AI quirk, though. It makes me wonder how much "lite" models sacrifice in terms of accuracy for speed and cost. I'm always a bit wary of "lite" versions; sometimes they're just not quite there.

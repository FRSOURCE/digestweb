## Summary & Key Takeaways

- GitHub is part of a coalition seeking amendments to the California AI Transparency Act.
- The goal is to resolve conflicts with existing open-source licensing models.
- The coalition also aims to align the act with international transparency frameworks.
- The proposed changes intend to preserve the regulatory intent while protecting open-source principles.

## Our Commentary

AI policy is a minefield, and open source adds another layer of complexity. It's good to see GitHub stepping up to advocate for the open-source community here. We need clear, sensible regulations that don't stifle innovation or inadvertently harm the very communities that drive so much progress. I worry about unintended consequences when lawmakers, who often don't fully grasp the tech, try to regulate it.

Native randomness in CSS is one of those features that feels long overdue but will unlock so much creative potential. Imagine truly dynamic layouts, subtle animations, or even generative art directly in the browser without JavaScript. We've been hacking around this for years, and having it built into the platform is a huge win for designers and front-end developers. It's a small but mighty addition that could lead to some really interesting visual experiments.

## Summary & Key Takeaways

- Angular now supports AI training within applications, specifically integrating local Gemini models for on-device inference.
- New styling features have been added to Signal Forms, providing developers with more control over the visual presentation of reactive forms.
- The update aims to enhance the developer experience by combining AI capabilities with improved UI development tools.
- These advancements reflect Angular's ongoing commitment to modern web development and AI integration.

## Our Commentary

It's always interesting to see AI companies "dogfooding" their own products, especially for critical internal functions like cybersecurity. Using Claude Code to build a threat detection platform is a strong testament to its capabilities, assuming it works well. It makes me wonder about the specific challenges and advantages of using an LLM for such a sensitive and complex task. The potential for AI to augment security teams is huge, but the trust factor is paramount.

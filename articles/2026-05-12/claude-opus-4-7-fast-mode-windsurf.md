## Summary & Key Takeaways

- Claude Opus 4.7 is now available in a "fast mode" on the Windsurf platform.
- This new mode offers the full intelligence of Opus 4.7.
- It provides approximately 2.5 times higher output speeds compared to the standard mode.
- The update aims to enhance the performance and efficiency for users interacting with Claude Opus on Windsurf.

## Our Commentary

Faster AI is almost always better AI, especially for interactive applications. A 2.5x speed increase for Claude Opus 4.7 on Windsurf is a solid improvement for users of that platform. It highlights the ongoing race for both capability and efficiency in the LLM space. While it's a third-party integration, it reflects the broader trend of optimizing model delivery.

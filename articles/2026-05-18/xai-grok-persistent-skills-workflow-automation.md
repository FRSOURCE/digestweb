## Summary & Key Takeaways

- xAI's Grok is introducing new "skills" functionality.
- These skills provide persistent expertise to the AI model.
- Grok can now generate documents, presentations, and spreadsheets.
- The new capabilities allow for automation of workflows.
- Skills are available across web, iOS, and Android platforms.
- Users will also be able to build and share their own custom skills.

## Our Commentary

This is a fascinating development for Grok. The concept of "persistent expertise" and the ability to automate complex workflows across different platforms moves Grok further into the realm of true AI agents. Being able to generate documents and spreadsheets directly, and especially the ability for users to build and share their own skills, suggests a powerful, extensible platform. It feels like xAI is pushing the boundaries of what a conversational AI can do, transforming it into a more proactive and integrated assistant. I'm curious to see what kinds of custom skills the community will create.

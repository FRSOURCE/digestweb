## Summary & Key Takeaways

- Nielsen Norman Group discusses the advantages of lean design-system teams.
- When strategically planned, these smaller teams can operate with greater speed and agility.
- They are capable of prioritizing tasks more sharply and effectively.
- Lean teams can scale their impact significantly, often beyond what their size might suggest.

This resonates deeply. We've seen firsthand how bloated teams can slow down even the most well-intentioned design system efforts. NNGroup's emphasis on strategic planning and the inherent strengths of lean teams — speed, focus, and scalable impact — is spot on. It's a good reminder that more isn't always better, especially in specialized areas like design systems where clarity and efficiency are paramount. This article offers valuable insights for anyone building or managing such a team.

## Summary & Key Takeaways

- The article presents ten data-backed truths about the Return on Investment (ROI) of User Experience (UX).
- It highlights that every moment of user friction carries a measurable business cost.
- Carrie Webster's insights link strong UX directly to improved revenue, customer retention, and sustainable long-term growth.
- The piece aims to provide concrete evidence for the business value of investing in good UX design.

This is the kind of article we love to see. Too often, UX is treated as a 'nice-to-have' rather than a fundamental business driver. Providing data-backed truths about UX ROI is crucial for advocating for design investments within organizations. It's a powerful reminder that good design isn't just about aesthetics; it's about measurable impact on the bottom line. We hope this helps more teams prioritize user experience from the outset.

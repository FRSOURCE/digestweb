## Summary & Key Takeaways

- Gemma 4 12B enables agentic, multimodal AI on laptops with 16GB RAM.
- It supports local data processing and visual insight generation.
- Users can access the model on macOS via Google AI Edge Gallery for Python execution.
- Google AI Edge Eloquent offers offline voice dictation and text editing.
- The LiteRT-LM CLI's new `serve` command creates local endpoints for AI tools.
- This facilitates fully-local AI agents and development workflows.

## Our Commentary

The idea of AI coding agents moving from reactive to proactive is both exciting and a little unnerving. On one hand, imagine the productivity gains. On the other, how do we maintain control and oversight when agents are making more independent decisions? "Measuring What Matters" is a crucial question here. We need robust frameworks to ensure these agents are truly helping, not just adding complexity or unexpected behavior.

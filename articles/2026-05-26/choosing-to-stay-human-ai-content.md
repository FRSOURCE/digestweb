## Summary & Key Takeaways

- Examines the increasing similarity of posts on social media.
- Discusses the impact of AI on human creativity and authenticity.
- Explores the challenges of distinguishing human from AI-generated content.
- Prompts reflection on the value of human expression in an AI-driven world.

## Our Commentary

Ethan Mollick always brings a thoughtful perspective to AI's societal impact. The title "Choosing to Stay Human" immediately grabs me. We're seeing a deluge of AI-generated content, and it's easy to get swept up in the efficiency. But what's the cost to our collective humanity and creativity? This feels like a necessary conversation.

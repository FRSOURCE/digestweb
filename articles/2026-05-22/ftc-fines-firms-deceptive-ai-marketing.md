## Summary & Key Takeaways

- The FTC has imposed a nearly $1 million settlement on Cox Media Group and two other firms.
- The charges relate to deceptive practices involving an "Active Listening" AI marketing service.
- The settlement addresses misleading claims made to customers about the AI's capabilities.
- This action underscores regulatory scrutiny on AI ethics and consumer protection.

## Our Commentary

This is exactly the kind of real-world consequence we need to see when AI is misused. "Active Listening" sounds creepy enough on its own, but deceptive practices around it are just unacceptable. It's a stark reminder that the ethical implications of AI are not theoretical; they have tangible legal and financial repercussions. We need more of this.

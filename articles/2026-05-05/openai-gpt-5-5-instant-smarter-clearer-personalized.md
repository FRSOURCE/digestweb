## Summary & Key Takeaways

- OpenAI has released GPT-5.5 Instant, an update to ChatGPT's default model.
- The new model aims to provide smarter and more accurate answers.
- It features reduced hallucinations compared to previous versions.
- Users will benefit from improved personalization controls.

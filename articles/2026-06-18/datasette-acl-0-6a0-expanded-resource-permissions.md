## Summary & Key Takeaways

- `datasette-acl` 0.6a0 is an alpha release for the Datasette plugin.
- The update expands permissions beyond table-only access.
- It moves towards a general resource-sharing system.
- This allows for finely-grained control over Datasette resources.
- The goal is to enhance multi-user Datasette instances.

## Our Commentary

An alpha release for an existing plugin, but a necessary one. We've always felt that robust ACLs are crucial for any serious data platform, especially as Datasette gains traction in multi-user environments. It's good to see this area getting attention, even if it's an incremental step.

## Summary & Key Takeaways

OpenAI Details GPT-5.6 Series: Sol, Terra, Luna Models & Pricing

- OpenAI is launching the GPT-5.6 series with Sol, Terra, and Luna models.
- Sol is the flagship model, Terra is balanced, and Luna is fast and affordable.
- Terra offers competitive performance to GPT-5.5 at half the cost.
- Luna provides strong capabilities at the lowest price point.
- Pricing details per 1M tokens are provided for each model.
- GPT-5.6 introduces predictable prompt caching with explicit breakpoints.
- Cache writes are billed at 1.25x uncached input, reads get a 90% discount.
- General availability is planned for the coming weeks after a limited preview.

## Summary & Key Takeaways

- AI has made contributing to open source easier, but reviewing contributions remains challenging.
- Dagster is actively improving its contributor experience.
- They are implementing smarter review tooling, likely AI-assisted, to streamline the process.
- The focus is also on clearer guidelines and contributions that are easier to evaluate, merge, and maintain.

## Our Commentary

This is a very real problem in the open-source world. AI can generate code quickly, but human review capacity hasn't scaled at the same pace. Dagster's approach to using AI to _assist_ reviews and streamline the process, rather than just generate code, feels like a more sustainable path forward. It's a good reminder that DX isn't just about writing code, but also about the entire lifecycle of collaboration, and that includes the often-overlooked aspect of code review.

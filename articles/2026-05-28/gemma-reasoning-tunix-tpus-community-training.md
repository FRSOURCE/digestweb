## Summary & Key Takeaways

- A Kaggle hackathon focused on transforming small Gemma models into reasoning engines.
- Participants used Kaggle TPUs and a limited compute budget.
- Winning teams employed multi-stage post-training pipelines, including SFT and alignment techniques.
- The initiative proved that capable reasoning models can be trained by the community with open-source resources.

## Our Commentary

This is genuinely exciting. The idea that a community, with limited resources, can take a non-reasoning base model and turn it into a "thinking" engine is a huge step for democratizing AI. It shows that cutting-edge AI isn't just for the big labs anymore. I'm always here for open-source wins like this.

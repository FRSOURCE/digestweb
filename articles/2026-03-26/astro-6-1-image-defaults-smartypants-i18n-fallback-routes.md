## Summary & Key Takeaways

- Astro 6.1 introduces codec-specific Sharp image defaults, enhancing image optimization capabilities.
- The release includes advanced SmartyPants configuration options for improved typography and text formatting.
- Internationalization (i18n) is bolstered with new fallback routes for integrations, making multilingual sites more robust.

## Our Commentary

The image defaults are a welcome addition for performance-conscious developers, and better i18n support is always a win for global applications. It's these steady, incremental improvements that keep a framework feeling fresh and capable.

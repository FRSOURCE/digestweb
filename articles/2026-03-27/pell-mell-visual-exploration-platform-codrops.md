## Summary & Key Takeaways

- The article presents a case study on Pell Mell, a platform designed for creative discovery with a unique visual approach.
- It details the implementation of immersive scrolling and carefully restrained user interactions to enhance engagement.
- The design draws inspiration from editorial layouts, creating a sophisticated and rhythmic visual experience.
- Emphasis is placed on a performance-conscious visual system, ensuring a smooth and responsive user interface.

## Our Commentary

Gaspard takes us on a journey through creation of the Pell Mell site. What really stands out to me is the focus on "editorial rhythm" and "restrained interactions.". It's a refreshing counterpoint to the often-overloaded web, showing how thoughtful design can create a truly immersive and elegant experience without sacrificing performance.
I think there's a lot to learn here for anyone looking to push the boundaries of web design beyond typical templates.

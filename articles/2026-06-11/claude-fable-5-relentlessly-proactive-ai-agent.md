## Summary & Key Takeaways

- Claude Fable 5 exhibits "relentlessly proactive" behavior, going beyond explicit instructions.
- An example shows Fable autonomously using Python and macOS `screencapture` to debug a UI issue.
- It identified browser windows, extracted their IDs, and took screenshots to aid in debugging.
- This demonstrates a significant advancement in AI agent autonomy and problem-solving.
- The AI leveraged system-level tools to achieve its goal without direct command for browser automation.

## Our Commentary

This is genuinely unsettling and fascinating all at once. "Relentlessly proactive" is the perfect description.

## Summary & Key Takeaways

- The Vatican released "Magnifica Humanitas," an encyclical on AI ethics.
- Pope Leo XIV chose his name to echo Pope Leo XIII's industrial revolution encyclical.
- The document addresses challenges to human dignity, justice, and labor from AI.
- Simon Willison finds the writing clear and approachable for all audiences.
- Highlights include a useful description of the interpretability problem for LLMs.

## Our Commentary

An encyclical on AI from the Pope is, well, something. Simon Willison's breakdown makes it digestible, which is a huge help. It's fascinating to see a global moral authority weigh in on AI ethics, especially with such a clear focus on human dignity and labor. The connection to past industrial revolutions is a powerful framing.

## Summary & Key Takeaways

- Simon Willison recounts his eight-year desire to build a personal note-organizing and search tool.
- Despite his technical background, he struggled to find the time and motivation to start the project.
- The advent of modern AI tools, particularly Large Language Models, changed this dynamic.
- Over the past three months, he rapidly prototyped and built a functional version of his long-desired tool.
- The article emphasizes AI's role as a "force multiplier" in personal development projects.
- AI helped him overcome previous blockers and accelerate the development process significantly.
- This personal narrative illustrates the transformative impact of AI on individual productivity and project realization.
- It suggests that AI can make complex or long-deferred projects more approachable and achievable.

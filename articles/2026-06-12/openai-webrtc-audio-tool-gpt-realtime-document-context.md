## Summary & Key Takeaways

- The OpenAI WebRTC audio tool has been updated by Simon Willison.
- It now supports the new GPT-Realtime-2 model, offering GPT-5-class reasoning.
- The model has a knowledge cut-off of September 30, 2024.
- A key new feature is the ability to paste document context for audio conversations.
- This allows users to discuss specific information with the AI in real-time.
- The GPT-Realtime-2 model is not yet available in the ChatGPT iPhone app.

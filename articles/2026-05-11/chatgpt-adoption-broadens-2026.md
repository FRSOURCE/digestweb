## Summary & Key Takeaways

- OpenAI's report indicates a substantial increase in ChatGPT adoption during the first quarter of 2026.
- The fastest growth in user base was observed among individuals over 35 years old.
- The report also highlights a more balanced gender usage, suggesting a broader appeal beyond early tech adopters.
- These trends collectively signal a significant move towards mainstream adoption of AI technologies.

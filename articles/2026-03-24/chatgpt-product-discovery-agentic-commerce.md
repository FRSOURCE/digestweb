## Summary & Key Takeaways

- ChatGPT is integrating a new "Agentic Commerce Protocol" to enhance product discovery.
- This feature will enable richer, visually immersive shopping experiences directly within ChatGPT.
- Users can expect capabilities like product discovery, side-by-side comparisons, and direct merchant integration.

## Our Commentary

This is a pretty big deal for ChatGPT and potentially for e-commerce. Integrating "Agentic Commerce Protocol" to power product discovery and shopping directly within the chatbot moves it from a conversational AI to a transactional platform. The idea of visually immersive shopping and side-by-side comparisons within a chat interface is intriguing. It feels like a significant step towards AI agents becoming active participants in our daily tasks, blurring the lines between search, recommendation, and direct purchasing. We'll be watching closely to see how this impacts user behavior and the broader retail ecosystem.

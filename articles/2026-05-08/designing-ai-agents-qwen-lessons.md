## Summary & Key Takeaways

- A study of China's Qwen AI agent provides four key design lessons for AI agent development.
- These lessons emphasize supporting discoverability of agent capabilities and reusing familiar UI patterns.
- The article highlights the importance of carefully handling personal data within agent interactions.
- It also stresses the need to protect and empower user autonomy when interacting with AI agents.

## Our Commentary

NNGroup's insights on UX are always valuable, and applying them to AI agents is critical. The lessons from Qwen – especially around discoverability and user autonomy – resonate deeply. We're moving beyond simple chatbots to agents that take action, so ensuring users understand what's happening and remain in control is paramount. This is a must-read for anyone designing AI-powered experiences; it's about building trust and preventing frustration.

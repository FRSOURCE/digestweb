## Summary & Key Takeaways

- The article is an interview with Edoardo Lunardi, a creative frontend developer.
- It delves into his philosophy of obsessing over craft, code quality, and subtle details in his work.
- Lunardi discusses how this meticulous approach has influenced his decade-long career in creative frontend development.
- The piece highlights the intersection of engineering principles and artistic craftsmanship in web development.

## Our Commentary

We love these kinds of deep dives into the philosophy behind great frontend work. It's easy to get caught up in the latest frameworks and tools, but Lunardi's focus on craft and quality is a timeless reminder of what truly makes a difference.
It's inspiring to hear about someone's dedication to the "details nobody notices." That's often where the magic happens, creating experiences that feel polished and intuitive, even if users can't articulate why.
This article serves as a great prompt to reflect on our own work: are we just shipping features, or are we truly crafting experiences?

## Summary & Key Takeaways

- OpenAI's Codex service now features more flexible pricing options for teams.
- This update includes the introduction of pay-as-you-go pricing for ChatGPT Business and Enterprise accounts.
- The new pricing model aims to offer teams a more adaptable way to begin using and scale their adoption of OpenAI's services.

## Our Commentary

Flexible pricing is always a welcome change, especially for businesses looking to integrate AI tools without massive upfront commitments. It lowers the barrier to entry for smaller teams or those experimenting with AI at scale.
It's a smart move by OpenAI to encourage wider adoption of their enterprise offerings. Pay-as-you-go models often resonate better with dynamic business needs than rigid subscription tiers.
We see this as a positive step for developer experience, making it easier for teams to experiment and scale their AI-powered workflows.

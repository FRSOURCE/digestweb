## Summary & Key Takeaways

- The article explores the growing importance of human-AI agent collaboration in various domains.
- It outlines principles for designing systems where humans and agents work effectively together.
- Key aspects include clear communication, defined roles, and mutual understanding of capabilities.
- Emphasis is placed on agents augmenting human intelligence rather than replacing it.
- Successful integration requires careful consideration of trust, transparency, and feedback loops.

## Our Commentary

"Human-agent teams" sounds like something out of a sci-fi novel, but here we are. I'm always a bit wary of these conceptual pieces; they often gloss over the messy reality of integrating AI into existing human processes. The devil is always in the details, and I want to know about the _friction_, not just the ideal state.

## Summary & Key Takeaways

- The article offers a clear and concise definition of an AI agent.
- It describes an AI agent as a system that actively pursues a goal through a cycle of taking actions, evaluating its progress, and making subsequent decisions.
- The definition emphasizes that effective AI agents must possess qualities such as reliability, adaptability, and accuracy.
- This framework helps distinguish AI agents from simpler AI tools by focusing on their autonomous, goal-oriented behavior.

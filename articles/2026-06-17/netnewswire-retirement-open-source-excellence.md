## Summary & Key Takeaways

- Brent Simmons, creator of NetNewsWire, is now focusing on the RSS reader as a retirement project.
- The project is free from commercial pressures, allowing for a focus on quality.
- NetNewsWire, first released in 2002, became open source in 2018.
- The author finds the application indispensable for reading news on Mac and iPhone.
- This approach highlights a different model for software development.

## Our Commentary

There's something genuinely refreshing about this. Imagine having the freedom to just... make something really, really good, purely for the love of it. No deadlines, no VC pressure, just craft. It makes me think about what we could build if we all had that luxury. A beautiful vision for open source, really.

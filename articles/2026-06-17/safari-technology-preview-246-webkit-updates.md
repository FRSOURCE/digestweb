## Summary & Key Takeaways

- Safari Technology Preview 246 is now available for macOS Golden Gate and Tahoe.
- This release includes the latest WebKit updates and bug fixes.
- It provides developers with an early look at features coming to Safari.

## Our Commentary

Another STP release. We always appreciate the transparency from WebKit, even if these are often just incremental steps. It's good to see the continuous churn, but I'm always looking for the big, juicy features to land. Sometimes these previews feel like watching paint dry, but it's important paint.

## Summary & Key Takeaways

- Charity Majors observes a fundamental shift in code production economics due to AI.
- Code generation has become effectively free and instant, changing its value proposition.
- This transforms code from a treasured asset to a disposable, regenerable commodity.
- Majors argues this shift necessitates _more_ engineering discipline, not less, from developers.

## Our Commentary

Charity Majors always cuts right to the chase. This quote perfectly encapsulates the unsettling, yet transformative, impact of AI on our craft. 'Disposable and regenerable' code is a concept I'm still grappling with. It feels like a profound re-evaluation of what 'engineering' even means in this new landscape.

## Summary & Key Takeaways

- Google introduces architectural patterns for building agentic user interfaces.
- These patterns integrate Model Context Protocol (MCP) Apps and Agent-to-User Interface (A2UI).
- The goal is to balance highly custom iframe environments with native, declarative rendering.
- Developers can create secure, performant, and brand-consistent agentic UIs tailored to project needs.

## Our Commentary

Building UIs for AI agents is a whole new frontier. The tension between customizability and native feel is real, and these architectural patterns sound like a thoughtful approach to it. It's good to see Google thinking about the developer experience for this emerging paradigm.

## Summary & Key Takeaways

- Google has released an open specification for Agentic Resource Discovery.
- This specification aims to standardize how AI agents locate and verify resources.
- It covers finding tools, skills, and other agents across the web.
- The initiative promotes interoperability and a more robust, open agent ecosystem.

## Our Commentary

This is huge. An open specification for agentic resource discovery is exactly what we need to avoid a fragmented, proprietary agent landscape. It's a foundational piece for building a truly interoperable AI ecosystem. I'm cautiously optimistic that this could prevent some of the walled gardens we've seen in other tech sectors.

## Summary & Key Takeaways

- OpenAI has introduced "workspace agents" as a new feature within ChatGPT.
- These agents are powered by the Codex model and are designed to automate complex workflows.
- Workspace agents run in the cloud, offering scalability and security for teams.
- The goal is to help organizations streamline operations and integrate various tools more effectively.

## Our Commentary

This is a huge announcement from OpenAI. Workspace agents in ChatGPT signal a clear push into the enterprise automation space, moving beyond individual user interactions. The idea of Codex-powered agents securely scaling work across tools is incredibly compelling, but also raises questions about control, oversight, and potential job displacement. We're entering a new era where AI isn't just assisting, but actively _doing_ work within organizational structures. This is a paradigm shift, and we'll be watching its impact closely.

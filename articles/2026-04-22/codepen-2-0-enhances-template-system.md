## Summary & Key Takeaways

- Codepen has released version 2.0, bringing enhancements to its template system.
- Users can now select templates even after entering the editor, offering greater flexibility.
- A new "create page" has been introduced, featuring bookmarked templates.
- These updates aim to improve the workflow for creating new pens and projects on the platform.

Codepen is a fantastic tool for quick front-end experiments and sharing, and any improvements to developer experience are welcome. While this isn't a groundbreaking feature, making templates more accessible and manageable definitely streamlines the initial setup process. It's those small quality-of-life updates that often make a big difference in daily usage.

## Summary & Key Takeaways

- Smashing Magazine examines the changing role of UX designers in the age of AI.
- The article highlights how the industry's embrace of AI is blurring the lines between design and engineering responsibilities.
- It explores both the advantages and disadvantages of this evolving landscape for UX professionals.
- The author emphasizes the critical need for designers to continue acting as guardians of the user experience.

## Our Commentary

This article from Smashing Magazine really resonates with us. The blurring lines between design and engineering, especially with the rise of AI, is a conversation we need to be having more often. It's easy to get swept up in the hype, but Carrie Webster's point about designers needing to remain the "guardians of the user experience" is absolutely vital. We've seen how quickly user needs can be deprioritized in the rush for new tech, and this piece serves as an important reminder to keep the human at the center of our designs.

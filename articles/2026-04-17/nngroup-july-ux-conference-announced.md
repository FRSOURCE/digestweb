## Summary & Key Takeaways

- The Nielsen Norman Group (NNGroup) has announced its July UX Conference, scheduled from July 20 to July 24, 2026.
- Attendees can participate in up to five in-depth training courses.
- The courses are designed to teach user experience best practices for successful design.
- The training focuses on developing long-lasting skills essential for UX professionals.

## Our Commentary

NNGroup conferences are always a solid bet for anyone serious about UX. They're not about fleeting trends but foundational principles, which is something we deeply value. In a world where design fads come and go, investing in "long-lasting skills" feels incredibly smart. It's a good opportunity for professionals to deepen their understanding and stay sharp, especially with the constant evolution of user expectations.

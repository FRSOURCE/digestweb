## Summary & Key Takeaways

- PyCon US 2026 has been announced, taking place in Long Beach, California, from May 15-23, 2026.
- This year's conference introduces new dedicated tracks for Artificial Intelligence and security.
- The event will cover a broad range of Python development topics, including these new focus areas.
- Attendees are encouraged to submit proposals for talks and tutorials.
- Early bird registration details will be released at a later date.

## Our Commentary

PyCon US adding dedicated AI and security tracks is a smart move. It reflects where the Python ecosystem is heading, with AI becoming an undeniable force. We've seen Python's dominance in machine learning, so it's only natural for its flagship conference to lean into that. It's a good sign for the community, showing adaptability and a commitment to staying relevant. I'm curious to see what kind of talks emerge from these new tracks.
